# hurdlebb 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Two-part hurdle beta-binomial model via CmdStan.
* Four model variants: base, weighted, SVC, SVC + weighted.
* Survey-weighted pseudo-posterior inference.
* Sandwich variance estimation and Cholesky posterior recalibration.
* Average marginal effects with extensive/intensive margin decomposition.
* LOO-CV model comparison and posterior predictive checks.
* Three built-in synthetic datasets based on NSECE 2019.
* Five vignettes covering full analysis workflow.
